About Me

Anthony W. Harrison

I am currently completing a combined PhD / Masters of Psychology (Clinical) at the University of New South Wales. Under the supervision of Professor Thomas Whitford, my research examines neural markers of the brain's capacity to distinguish sensations that have been self-generated from those created externally.

In addition to research, I am enthusiastic to share my interest in perception, cognition, statistics and psychopathology through teaching. I'm pleased to share these recent student evaluations of my tutorials.

I am also a practising psychologist, with experience working in the service of patients presenting with a range of severe mental disorders. This has including tertiary referral services specialising in OCD and anxiety, as well as child and adolescent mental health.
